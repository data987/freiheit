Heydays is a Norwegian design agency based in Oslo. The focus of our work is developing visual identities, digital solutions and other associated material.

How we work

We use insight, instinct and commitment in our design work and dare to challenge the established truth. Our conviction is that the most precise solutions are found together with our clients and we embrace an open and passionate dialogue. Each of our designers work directly with their respective client contacts for easy and tight communication throughout the project.

A key asset beside our own team and knowledge, is a well-developed network of other talented people. Photographers, copywriters, advertisers, animators, 3D artists and illustrators. We know who would fit your project.

Five years on, Heydays is one of the most award winning graphic design agencies in Norway.
